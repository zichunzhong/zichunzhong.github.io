Short Bio:
    Dr. Zichun Zhong is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Wayne State University (WSU). He was an Assistant Professor at WSU from August 2015 to July 2020. Prior to joining WSU, he was a postdoctoral fellow in Department of Radiation Oncology at UT Southwestern Medical Center at Dallas (UTSW) from August 2014 to August 2015. He received Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas (UTD) in Summer 2014. He received B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology (Software Engineering) and M.S. degree in Computer Science at The University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2006 and 2009, respectively. He received the NSF CAREER Award in 2019, NSF CRII Award in 2017, College of Engineering Faculty Research Excellence Award at WSU in 2019, Certificate of Academic Excellence at UTD in 2013, and College of Engineering Award for Excellence in Teaching at WSU in 2020.
Research Interests:

    Dr. Zhong's research interests focus on Geometric Modeling and Computing for Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, Visualization, Medical Image Processing, Machine Learning, and GPU Algorithms.

Prospective Students:

    He is looking for self-motivated Master thesis and Ph.D. students to join his research group. Fellowship / RA / TA positions with full scholarships are available. If you are interested in working with him, please send him your CV and related projects / publications.
